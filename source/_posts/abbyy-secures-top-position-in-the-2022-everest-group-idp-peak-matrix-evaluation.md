Organizations worldwide rely on the IDP PEAK Matrix to evaluate the best solutions that understand business documents and find meaning in a human-like manner.

The PEAK Matrix is a proprietary framework for assessing the relative market success and overall capability of service providers based on performance, experiences, ability, and knowledge. Each service provider is comparatively assessed on two dimensions: market success and delivery capabilities. ABBYY’s market success was measured by revenue, number of clients and year-over-year growth, and delivery capability was measured by scale of operations, scope, technology and innovation, delivery footprint and buyer satisfaction. Everest Group analysts also interviewed ABBYY customers.

ABBYY has consistently positioned high for its impact on the market and ability to deliver product successfully.
